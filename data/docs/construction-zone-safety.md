# Construction Zone Safety

## Purpose

This document defines baseline safety requirements for lunar habitat construction and expansion work.

## Preconditions

- Verify that the day's construction task has an assigned operator.
- Confirm that the work zone has been inspected within the last 12 hours.
- Confirm that pressure-stable adjacent modules are not scheduled for simultaneous intrusive maintenance.

## Expansion Work Rules

- Expansion scaffold work must be preceded by a structural inspection.
- Regolith shield printer activity must not begin until dust barriers are deployed.
- Any task involving exterior shell modification is high priority if it affects radiation shielding continuity.

## Monitoring Requirements

- Record start and stop times for each construction task.
- Log vibration, dust, and seal-status readings at the start of the task window.
- Pause work immediately if seal readings drift outside the approved threshold.

## Conflict Warnings

- Do not schedule construction drilling during a crew communications uplink unless the uplink is marked non-critical.
- Do not overlap scaffold inspection with emergency egress drills.

## Escalation

- If a structural inspection is missing, block the construction task and request manual review.
- If shielding continuity is uncertain, elevate the task to mission-critical status.
